Research Lab & Team Philosophy

Building world-class AI teams

Technical Leadership Approach

I lead by example, staying deeply involved in technical work while guiding my team of 23 researchers. In a field where hundreds of papers are published daily on ArXiv, I curate information flow through daily technical briefings, ensuring our team remains focused on relevant breakthroughs. This positions me as both a technical leader and mentor.

Research Factory Model

I've established a rigorous approach to research that operationalizes the process of discovery. Leading a distributed team of 23 individuals (7 direct reports and 16 remote contractors), I implement "Execution maps" and "Jira/Confluence" workflows to ensure efficient collaboration and project delivery.

Player-Coach Philosophy

As a technical leader, I maintain hands-on involvement in key projects. Even in 2025, I am listed as lead author or co-author on technical papers like Trick-gs. This "Player-Coach" model ensures I stay connected to the technical challenges while providing strategic direction.
